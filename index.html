Llamashot

A fast, lightweight screenshot and screen recording tool for Windows. Capture, annotate, record, and extract text.

WPF / .NET 10 Open Source Windows 10/11
Download Installer Download Standalone .exe Explore Features

Everything You Need

Screenshot, annotate, record, and extract text — all in one lightweight tool

📷

Region Capture

Press PrintScreen, drag to select any area. Dimmed overlay with crosshair cursor and live dimension display. Resize handles for pixel-perfect adjustment.

✏️

10 Annotation Tools

Pencil, line, arrow, rectangle, ellipse, text, marker, blur, plus check and cross stamps for review workflows. Configurable color and thickness.

⏺

Screen Recording

Record any selected region as MP4 video. Up to 2 minutes at 10fps with pause/resume. Red pulsing border shows the recorded area. Recording toolbar hidden from capture.

🔤

OCR Text Extraction

Extract text from any area of the screen. Drag a sub-region to target specific text. Auto-inverts dark backgrounds for better recognition. Uses Windows built-in OCR.

📋

Screenshot History

Thumbnail gallery of all saved and copied screenshots. Green "Saved" and blue "Copied" badges. One-click re-copy from history. Configurable storage location.

📌

Pin on Screen

Pin any screenshot as a floating always-on-top window. Adjustable opacity with mouse wheel. Drag to reposition, double-click to copy, right-click to dismiss.

Designed for Speed

Every detail crafted for a seamless workflow

⌨️

21 Customizable Shortcuts

Every tool and action has a keyboard shortcut. Click-to-record in Settings. Duplicate detection prevents conflicts. Single keys or combos supported.

🗂️

Smart Toolbar

Adaptive layout auto-switches between 1 and 2 columns based on screen height. Black background with white icons for maximum contrast. No buttons ever hidden.

⏱️

Delayed Capture

1, 3, 5, or 10 second countdown timer. Perfect for capturing menus, tooltips, and hover states that disappear on click.

💾

Multiple Output Formats

Save as PNG, JPG, or BMP. Configurable JPEG quality slider. Copy to clipboard with one click. All screenshots tracked in history.

🖥️

Multi-Monitor & DPI Aware

Captures across all connected displays. PerMonitorV2 DPI awareness for crisp rendering on high-DPI screens. Proper coordinate mapping.

↩️

Resize & Pan After Drawing

Selection handles stay active even after annotations. Drag to resize, Space+drag or V key to pan selection with all annotations together.
